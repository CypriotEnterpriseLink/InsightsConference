Yiannis Gavrielides is the CEO and co-founder of Covve, a professional address book and networking software. He is a director and co-founder of Invelopment Partners, a small-venture investor in Cyprus. In his corporate life he worked as a management consultant in London and a banker in Athens. He holds an MEng in Chemical Engineering from Imperial College London.
