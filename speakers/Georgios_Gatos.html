I love people with entrepreneurial spirit. This is why I love startups and technology oriented companies.

In 2012 I co founded incrediblue to offer hassle free boating holidays through an online marketplace, so that more travellers around the world can enjoy the best places on earth and create happy memories with those they love!

In the past I worked as a consultant of small and medium size innovative companies, helping them fundraise and/or narrow down the gap between a concept idea and its targeted market, while later I worked as an investment analyst and advisor of Venture Capital firms.

I actively participate in groups fostering entrepreneurship and innovation, business plan competitions and venture academies, curate the Open Coffee Thessaloniki meetings and belong to the TEDxThessaloniki founding team.
