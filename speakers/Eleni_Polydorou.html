Eleni Polydorou

Co-Founder & Creative Director Polydorou Design

Linkedin

Eleni Polydorou is a woman who is energetic, inspired and above all, dedicated to achieving the ambition and creative insight of her clients. In the world of branding, marketing and publishing, Eleni has built a reputation which is matched only by her passion and dedication.

With an eye for beauty and a mind for communication, she has supported a series of leading hotels on the island – including the Four Seasons Limassol, Amathus Hotels, Elysium Hotel and Louis Hotel Group – offering them everything from powerful handcrafted logos and full brand identities to the whole range of concept based collateral.

In addition, she has launched a series of bespoke in-house magazines. In her own sharp and insightful way, Eleni knows how to take a story and convert it into a visual fairytale with a hands-on approach to perfectly styled custom photography that ensures a unique and authentic story, every time.

Eleni is part of the husband and wife team of Polydorou Design, a brand and design communications consultancy. The Polydorou team have also been proud to win a number of industry awards including the “Best of Nation” award for logo design and various competitions and government-based commissions and included in international design publication for logo design – LogoLounge.
