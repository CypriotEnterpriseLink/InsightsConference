Marinos Cleanthous

Director IP Cyprus

@cleanthous Linkedin

Marinos is an intellectual property rights activist.

After his graduation from Thessaloniki University (LLB), he obtained his LLM from University of Leicester. He was a PhD researcher student at the University of Leicester. His research area covered European Intellectual Property Law.

He is an intellectual property attorney since 2005.

Since 2013 Marinos is a partner of IP CYPRUS Ioannides Cleanthous & Co LLC: a boutique law firm specialised in intellectual property law.

During the last years he also worked as a part-time lecturer at the University of Nicosia. He also presented papers about intellectual property rights in many legal conferences and seminars (in Cyprus and abroad) organised by Universities, Governmental bodies and Professional Organisations.

He is an active member of Cyprus Bar Association, of the International Intellectual Property Lawyers Association (IIPLA) and of International Trademark Association (INTA).

Marinos’s favourite quote is “everyone falls down. Only the best get back up”.
