Valadis Novakovits is a software engineer.

One year ago he joined Covve to build technology from scratch and deliver the first commercial version of the software. Nowadays, as a part of the core development team, he uses agile principles, patterns and practices, enjoys investigating technology solutions while trying to produce clean object oriented code. Valadis is delighted to face amazing challenges every day, such as implementing Covve’s automatic emails feature and performing the occasional bug fix.

He has been coding professionally since 2009. Valadis’ first projects were in the banking sector. His bachelor was in Informatics and Telecommunications department of Ethnikon kai Kapodistriakon Panepistimion Athinon. He used to tutor high school students on fundamental concepts of computer science while he was taking his masters in Information Systems from Athens University of Economics and Business Information Systems.
