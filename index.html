The Insights Conference 2015

aims to provide a mix of practical and theoretical knowledge to an audience hungry to learn. The conference will take place over two days and will include workshops and talks by world-class founders who will share their startup experience and expertise with our audience. The Insights Conference will take place on 28th-29th March 2015 at the Filoxenia Conference Centre, Nicosia, Cyprus.
