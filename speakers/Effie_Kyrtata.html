Effie Kyrtata

Community Development Manager, Reload Greece

@ekyrt Linkedin

Effie is one of the co-founders of Reload Greece, the hub of young entrepreneurs in London, which inspires and educates the global community of the diaspora to make a social and economic impact in Greece. Her MSc in Innovation, Entrepreneurship and Management at Imperial College in London combined with her need to connect to Greece while residing abroad, acted as her motivation to co-found Reload Greece with the other Reloaders where she now acts as the Community Development Manager.
