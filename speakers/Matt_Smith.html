Matt Smith is director of the Centre for Entrepreneurs - a London-based think tank that promotes the role of entrepreneurs in creating economic growth and social well-being. The Centre is a non-profit organisation chaired by serial entrepreneur Luke Johnson and housed in the Legatum Institute. It is supported by a prominent advisory board including lastminute.com co-founder Brent Hoberman, angel investor Dale Murray, Supper Club founder Duncan Cheatle and Betfair founder Ed Wray. The Centre is also the home of the UK's national enterprise campaign, StartUp Britain. Previously, Matt founded enterprise consultancy firm, Entelligence and co-founded the student enterprise charity, NACUE.
