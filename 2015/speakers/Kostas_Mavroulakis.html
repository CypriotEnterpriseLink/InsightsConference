Kostas Mavroulakis

Enterprise Champion, Start-up Advisor

@kostamavs Linkedin

Kostas is a passionate and hard-working enterprise champion, start-up adviser and digital expert that always goes out of his way to help people when he does and doesn't have time!

He has dedicated the past 7 years to supporting many young entrepreneurs and enterprise leaders through many enterprise promotion movements, events, initiatives and competitions. Most notably, he is one of the Founding Members & a former Executive Director (Digital) of the National Association of College and University Entrepreneurs (NACUE), a non-profit organisation that secured a multi-million pound investment from the UK Government's Department of Business Innovation and Skills (BIS) and contracts with Tata, Lloyds Bank and more . At NACUE, he supported its growth by growing its presence on-the-ground in the East of England, chaired its national student advisory board, developed most of its leader support resources used by 400 young enterprise leaders per year and transformed its digital strategy as its Digital Development Director. He continues to support many local, regional, national and international initiatives. These include judging business competitions like the UKTI (a government department) Sirius Programme for international graduate entrepreneurs, Shell's LiveWire Entrepreneur of the Year Awards as well as mentoring on enterprise programmes like the Start-up Generation Global Fellowship Programme and the DotForge Accelerator.

Kostas was born and lives in Birmingham in the UK to parents from Greece (Crete) and Cyprus (Larnaca) and speaks fluent Greek - spending time in London advising and sharing his experiences of NACUE with the the management teams of CEL and Reload Greece. He also continues to advise and support several start-ups including the teams behind Accredible.com, TubeNav.com, GZero and Dojit Games as well as supporting many aspiring young entrepreneurs through above initiatives. Furthermore, he completed his BSc (Hons) Computer and Business Studies and MSc Information Systems and Management degrees at one of the UK's leading universities, the University of Warwick and Warwick Business School.

If you need any help, please feel free to connect with him on Linkedin and where possible he will try to advise and/or connect you with who you need to help you address your current challenges.
