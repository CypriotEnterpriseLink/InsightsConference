Denis Constantinou

Co-Founder & Art Director Funifi

@ConstantinouD Linkedin

Denis studied Animation Production at the Arts Institute at Bournemouth and then completed his Master's degree in 3D Animation at Bournemouth University. He has worked as an animator and designer on series for CBeebies, advertisments for Nickelodeon and contributed work for Disney XD.

He is currently the Art Director and a co-founder at Funifi.
