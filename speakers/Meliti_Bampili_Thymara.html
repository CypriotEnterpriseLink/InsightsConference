Meliti Bampili-Thymara

Co-Founder / Events & Projects Manager, Reload Greece

Linkedin

Meliti in amongst the co-founders of Reload Greece, the hub of young entrepreneurs in London, which inspires and educates the global community of the diaspora to make a social and economic impact in Greece. Having a degree in Architecture and a genuine interest in the creative and cultural industries, she is now undertaking an MA in Culture, Policy and Management at City University London and acts as a part-time Events & Project Manager at the Reloaders' team.
