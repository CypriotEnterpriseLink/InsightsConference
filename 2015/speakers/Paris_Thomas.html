Paris Thomas

Co-Founder Open Box Communication, ENERMAP, Chrysalis LEAP

@thomasparis Linkedin

Paris is a passionate communicator and a serial entrepreneur. He holds a BA in Communication Science, an MBA in Strategic Management, and is one of 30 globally certified trainers on Osterwalder’s groundbreaking Business Model Generation methodology. His background is comprised of serving major Canadian organisations at senior management level, lobbying government, and facilitating and training throughout Europe, North America, and the Middle East.

He is a co-founding partner at Open Box Communication, a boutique management consulting firm based in Cyprus, a co-founder at ENERMAP (a cleantech startup dealing with the energy efficiency of buildings) and co-founder and director at Chrysalis LEAP (Cyprus’ first accelerator platform).

Paris focuses on delivering results in a rolled-up sleeves type of approach and his favourite motto is “Laugh at people that take themselves too seriously”
